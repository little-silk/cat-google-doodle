My Topic Google Doodle

I decided to make a google doodle on cats. I was inspired by my three kitties at home and wanted to create a doodle in honor of them, so each kitty makes an appearance in my doodle. YumYum is a chocolate point siamese mix, he’s the cat sitting on the red O scratching himself. Maya is the grey and white domestic longhair, she’s sleeping on the lowercase G swinging her tail. My last cat is Mr. Mambo, he’s lounging on the E.
